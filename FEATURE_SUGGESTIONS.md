## ğŸ“ Strategic Ad Placement Locations

### Non-Intrusive Ad Zones (Implemented)

1. **Sidebar Bottom** (Mobile-friendly)
   - Small banner ad
   - Doesn't interfere with navigation
   - Only visible when scrolling

2. **Dashboard Right Sidebar** (Desktop)
   - Vertical banner
   - Appears after main content
   - Collapsible option

3. **Between Content Sections**
   - Horizontal banner between cards
   - Natural content break
   - Easy to scroll past

4. **Top Bar Sponsored Section**
   - Small text/link ad
   - Minimal space usage
   - Non-blocking

5. **Modal/Overlay Bottom**
   - Small banner in modals
   - After main content
   - Doesn't block actions

### Ad Placement Best Practices

âœ… **DO:**
- Place ads in natural content breaks
- Use collapsible/dismissible ads
- Make ads clearly labeled as "Sponsored"
- Ensure ads don't block critical actions
- Use responsive ad sizes
- Test on mobile devices

âŒ **DON'T:**
- Place ads over main content
- Use pop-up ads during study sessions
- Block navigation or buttons
- Use auto-playing video ads
- Place ads in timer/critical features
- Interrupt user workflows
